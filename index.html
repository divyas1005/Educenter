About Our College

Since 1983, D Y Patil Group of Institutions is leading group known amongst the top education institutions in India. The D Y Patil University, Pune was established by and under the Government of Maharashtra Act No. VI of 2019 and recognized by UGC, New Delhi, India D Y Patil Group of Institutions is founded to provide quality education and contribute towards the social responsibility. The foundation was laid by Father Founder Hon’ble Dr D Y Patil in the year 1983 by starting the first engineering college in Navi Mumbai. Our Course

Automotive Engineer

Automotive engineering is considered as one of the most rewarding, exciting and challenging careers. The job of an automotive engineer is to do the necessary research, design and finally develop different vehicles. They also get the opportunity to work with some of the most sophisticated technologies and bring the freedom of mobility to the world by creating various products./p>

Computer Engineering

The job of Computer Engineers is to develop and improve the software programs and hardware that makes the working of computers better and more efficient. Computer engineers can do their specialization in either software or hardware. The most significant benefit that a career in computer engineering offers is that it includes a high pay-roll, job security and an innovative environment that helps bring the best out of you.

Civil Engineering

Civil engineering is one of the most preferred engineering streams. The responsibility of a civil engineer is to design and construct roads, bridges, buildings and water supply systems for the public use. They supervise all the work and construction teams while working with other engineers.

Electrical Engineering

The key responsibilities of an electrical engineer are to design, test, develop and supervise the manufacturing of electrical equipment. They are also trained to handle other responsibilities such as the installation of wiring and lighting in a building, automobiles or aircraft.

Drafting and design Engineering

It is a very exciting and innovative career option that gives an opportunity to the engineer to be involved in every stage of the designing process. Their responsibility includes preparing blueprints or models of various structures or products.

Software Engineering

Software engineering is a highly-paid career as compared to other careers. The job of a software engineer is to perform all the necessary tests, prepare the design, and to develop and maintain computer software. They are responsible for programming computers so that they operate efficiently.
